Assume Nothing

Treat all factual claims made by an AI as unverified until proven otherwise.

Multiple Sources

Do not rely on a single source, even if the AI cites one. Cross-reference information across several reputable and independent sources.

Primary Sources

Prioritise primary sources (original research papers, artefacts, assets, government reports, official statistics) over secondary sources (news articles summarising research, blog posts).

Check Data

Verify the recency of information. AI's knowledge cut-off might be several months or years old.
